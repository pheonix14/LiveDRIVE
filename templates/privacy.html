PRIVACY POLICY

Absolute Anonymity, Zero Tracking, Client-First.

1. No Accounts, No Credentials

LiveDRIVE does not offer logins, sign-ups, or user accounts. There are no usernames, passwords, or emails stored on any backend database. Every visitor is completely anonymous. A random nickname (e.g. GhostReel_447) is generated locally upon your first visit.

2. Local-Only Storage

All persistent configurations—such as your randomly-generated identity, room access lists, chat logs, customized control settings (loop modes, shuffle states), and playlist queues—are written directly to your browser's localStorage. This information never leaves your device unless broadcast to peers in your current theater room.

3. Real-Time Peer Synchronization

When inside a theater room, playback synchronization commands (play, pause, seek, buffering states, and chat messages) are relayed in real-time through standard WebSocket channels or BroadcastChannel (for same-device loops). The signaling server functions purely as a message dispatcher and does not write message contents, chat history, or room settings to disk.

4. Third-Party Media Sources

LiveDRIVE allows users to play videos hosted on YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive, and other streaming targets. When using these sources, you are subject to the respective platform's privacy policy and data collection protocols. LiveDRIVE itself does not track what videos are loaded.

5. Policy Modifications

Because we do not store emails or contact information, we cannot notify users of privacy updates. You can check back on this page periodically to verify the terms of our client-side architecture.
